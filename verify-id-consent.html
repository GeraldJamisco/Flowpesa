What is Flowpesa?

Flowpesa is a financial services platform built to help people send, save and transact securely across Africa. To keep everyone safe and comply with KYC/AML laws, we need to verify your identity.

How we use your data

  • We use your document photos/videos and, if needed, a short face selfie video (“Scan Data”) only to verify you, prevent fraud, and meet legal requirements.
  • Scan Data is kept for up to 360 days after submission (or less if regulations require). After that it’s permanently deleted unless the law requires us to keep it longer.
  • Document photos/videos and audio are stored for up to 3 years and then destroyed, unless a longer period is legally required.
  • Your data is encrypted and access is restricted to a small team that needs it for verification.
  • You can request deletion where permitted by law. See our Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions.

By choosing “Accept”, you consent to this use of your data to verify your identity for your Flowpesa account.
